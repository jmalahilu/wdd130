Rafter's Edge: The Adventure Starts Here!

Our mission is simple: to connect people with the natural world through exhilarating white water adventures. We prioritize safety, sustainability, and sharing smiles! Join us for a trip you'll never forget.

History

Founded in 1985 by a group of passionate river guides, Rafter's Edge has grown from a single raft and a dream into a respected leader in adventure tourism. Our dedication to preserving the pristine beauty of our rivers is matched only by our commitment to providing unforgettable experiences for every guest. We've navigated thousands of miles and shared the thrill of the rapids with generations of adventurers.
