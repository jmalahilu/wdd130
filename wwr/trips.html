Family Adventure Package

Duration: 3 hours

Difficulty: Beginner (Class I-II)

Perfect for families with children ages 5 and up! This gentle float down scenic river sections is ideal for creating memories together. Our experienced guides ensure safety while you enjoy the beautiful scenery and wildlife viewing opportunities.

Intermediate Explorer Experience

Duration: 5 hours

Difficulty: Intermediate (Class II-III)

For those with some rafting experience, this thrilling journey combines exciting rapids with stunning natural scenery. You'll navigate challenging whitewater sections while learning advanced paddling techniques from our expert guides. Includes lunch and swimming breaks.

Extreme Adrenaline Challenge

Duration: 7 hours

Difficulty: Advanced (Class IV-V)

This is not for the faint of heart! Experience the most intense whitewater adventure with Class IV and V rapids that will test your courage and skills. Limited to experienced rafters only. Full safety briefing and premium equipment provided.
